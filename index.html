The Art Map 2026

Tropical Curated Modernism

The Tobago Art Map is a curated guide to the island's vibrant creative ecosystem. From the oldest protected rainforest in the Western Hemisphere to the bustling streets of Scarborough, we celebrate the artists, galleries, and heritage sites that make Tobago unique.

This digital hub serves as an interactive extension of our physical map, providing deeper insights, exact locations, and direct connections to the people and places that define Tobago's cultural landscape.

Our design philosophy, Tropical Curated Modernism, balances the organic beauty of the Caribbean with a clean, structured aesthetic, reflecting the island's dual nature as both a natural paradise and a historical crossroads.
